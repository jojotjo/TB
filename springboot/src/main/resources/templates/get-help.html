❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a ticket? ▼
Click on "Search Trains" from the home page, enter your source and destination, select your preferred train, choose seats, and complete the booking. You'll receive a confirmation immediately.
Can I cancel my booking? ▼
Yes, you can cancel bookings up to 24 hours before the train departure. Visit "My Bookings" and click the cancel button. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
How do I download my e-ticket? ▼
Go to "My Bookings", find your confirmed booking, and click the "Download Ticket" button. The PDF will be saved to your device automatically.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, net banking, mobile wallets (Google Pay, Paytm), and UPI payments. All payments are secured with 256-bit encryption.
Is there a minimum age for booking? ▼
You must be at least 18 years old to book tickets independently. Children under 5 travel free, and ages 5-12 get a 50% discount.
How far in advance can I book? ▼
You can book up to 60 days in advance for most trains. Some special trains may have different booking windows. Check while searching for availability.
